The "spanner" domain is a planning problem involving a man, named Bob, who needs to tighten some loose nuts using spanners. The domain includes objects like spanners, nuts, and locations, and has actions to walk between locations, pick up spanners, and tighten nuts. The example problem, "prob0", describes a situation where Bob needs to navigate through a series of locations, pick up the appropriate spanners, and use them to tighten three loose nuts located at a gate. The goal is to have all the nuts tightened.
